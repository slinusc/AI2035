# AI Futures 2035

Welcome. This interface helps you design and iteratively refine **positive
future scenarios for a world with artificial intelligence by 2035**.

## How it works

You provide only two things yourself:

1. **Background of your assumptions** — the perspective you are thinking from.
2. **Your assumptions** (about 3–10) — one per line.

The goal and the work assignment are already configured. After your inputs you
receive a first scenario draft and can then **continue the conversation
freely**, ask follow-up questions, and sharpen the scenario together.

Your session is saved and can be reopened from the left sidebar at any time.
